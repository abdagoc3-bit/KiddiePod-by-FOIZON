Our Vision & Mission

Our Vision

At Foizon, we promise to make every household a beautiful classroom space. Through our app KiddiePod, we give toddlers fun science-themed story books to easily listen to!

Our Mission

By listening to creative audiobooks about nature, cute biology, and modern inventions, small kids expand their brain potential and easily build their own dreams!
